About

Check out how we came to be!

  • 2011-2013

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Some time ago back in high school, I had an idea to create a company. At first it was just something simple, with not much to offer, but one day during a school pre game the principle had asked me to play music for the event. This was the start of a wonderful beginning.

  • May 2013

    An Agency is Born

    Ever since then I was addicted to music. I always wanted to listen to something whether it be new radio hits, fresh music off the block, or the old school music that laid the bricks for the music industry. From there with the help of my family, a small DJ company was starting.

  • December 2012

    Transition to Full Service

    From there it was mostly researching how to get everything in order to start a DJ company. Starting with small family events, I quickly gained clients through word of mouth and started picking up small gigs. From there it got bigger and bigger, doing full scale weddings and venues across the Pueblo location.

  • July 2014

    Expansion

    Now that I've gained enough experience, I believe I'am ready to take on the world. With this new website, and business profiles across the social media industry, we can make the world a little better with music at your next big event.
